BROWN'S SHOE FIT CO.

Finding a comfortable shoe shouldn't be a guessing game. When you're looking for the right fit, you need someone experienced to help you find the shoes you need. In business since 1911, Brown's Shoe Fit Co. has always focused on shoes that fit well and provide superior comfort, support, and style.

About Us

We Know How Our Brands Fit You

When you need a shoe that fits your foot perfectly, come to Brown's Shoe Fit Co. to have your footprint scanned. There's no need to purchase a one-size-fits-all brand or make a hit and miss attempt at cutting a shape to fit your shoe. Our system and selection allow us to fit you into the shoe you want.

Our B.I.O.system shoes are ideal for people with plantar fasciitis and other ailments. Discover the comfort of customization by visiting Brown's Shoe Fit Co. today.

The Best Brands

We do whatever it takes to keep our shelves stocked with the shoes you want. With a wide selection of the best brands it's easy to find the shoe for you. If for some reason we don't have what you need, we'll place an order and have it as soon as possible.

The Best Service

We take pride in having some of the best customer service staff in the midwest. Our goal is to make sure you can come in, find what you need, and have you walking out the door in a perfect fitting pair of shoes as quickly as possible.

A Personalized Experience

When someone enters our store, we make sure every interaction is tailored to them. Not only will we get you what you need, we'll get it to you in a way you prefer.
